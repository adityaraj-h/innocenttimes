Rama Shukla

Child’s name:
Akshit Narula, Senior KG



We relocated to Pune in 2016. There were several challenges ahead of us: I started working after a long break after Akshit was born, Akshit was barely 3 years old and Pune was a new city for myself and my husband. Being working parents it was needed that our child's education must not be compromised and he must get home-like care after school in our absence.

After searching and evaluating many schools in the vicinity, we were convinced that Innocent Times is the best choice. Akshit completed his Junior KG and Senior KG from Innocent Times plus joined their daycare.

The activities you planned, the way you taught was all incredible. It helped Askhit to settle down and realize that school is fun. He was lucky to have teachers like Shabnam and Minal who gave Akshit the foundation which I am sure he will cherish throughout his life. The support staff specially Ashwini Mausi and Bharti Mausi are fabulous. They involve themselves with each child to provide the comfort which we believed was possible at home only.

The best part of the school was open communication which happened via coordinators, diaries, notices, emails, etc. Proactive feedbacks are taken from parents and are actioned upon which is a differentiator. School coordinator Shweta is fabulous. She ensured all administrative issues are ironed out with parents. Last but not least, Ankita's leadership is outstanding.

Child’s name:
Aarna Shukla



My daughter Aarna Shukla joined as a 9 month kid and we started to send her to the daycare facility of innocent Times.Initially, we used to send her for 2 hours only and gradually as she grew we started to increase daycare hours. My surprise, Aarna never showed any separation anxiety, this was a very pleasant experience for us as parents.Every day she looks forward to coming to daycare.

Innocent Times not only took care of her but also engaged her in various activities.Every other day she uses to bring her drawing or paper cuttings at home and would talk for hours on what happened in the daycare, what she eats, when she slept and about her friends.I would also like to mention here that Innocent Times had played a very critical role in developing my daughter’s language, skills, motor skills and caring and sharing skills.

As a parent, we are really happy to be associated with Innocent Times, with happy and blissful daycare experience the logical decision for us was to send Aarna to Innocent Times.

The biggest factor for us to consider for Aarna while selecting a school is her safety, which Innocent Times provides and hence parent’s anxieties are taken care of equally. I also found teachers of Innocent Times highly qualified, patience and very present. Staff is also very courteous and service oriented, which is a bonus.

I recommend Innocent Times to all new parents. Wishing the Innocent Times family all the best for their future.
