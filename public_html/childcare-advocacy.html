Childcare Advocacy

The first five years are critical for the child’s proper development. It is when neurons in their brains are rapidly making connections and interpreting things. This is when a child is understanding the world around him or her and learning to learn. It has been shown in research that early childhood education and a positive environment help the child enjoy proper growth, confidence, and success in future life.
