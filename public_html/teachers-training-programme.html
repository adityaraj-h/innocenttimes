COURSE OVERVIEW

Pre-primary Teachers’ Training Programme is developed to groom and prepare skillful and creative teachers by combining intelligence and humanity with enthusiasm and love.

The curriculum includes the latest trends and developments in teacher education.

Moreover, it also provides you exposure to opportunities in a different environment with hands-on training, internships, and research visits to special schools.

The course is provided by Innocent Times with Sunderji Teachers’ Training Institute.

WHY TRAINING PROGRAMME

The course will educate you in all areas of pre-primary teaching and handling the kids with care.

On completion, you’ll be given a certificate recognised by N.C.T.E. making you eligible for job nationally, also preparing you to start your own pre-school.

BENEFITS OF TRAINING PROGRAMME

Job opportunities with national, international, kindergarten schools, and tuition, hobby classes.
