Making their first school memories fascinating

Our Kindergarten Programmes are thoughtfully designed to facilitate children’s overall development. We are dedicated to providing international level quality education and exposure to children. While we educate our students, we simultaneously teach ourselves new methods, upgrade our knowledge, and invent innovative and exciting ways to engage children and change their perception towards education.

Our Programmes are supported and made even more impactful by our child-appropriate infrastructure, safe, familiar, and loving environment, and passionate teachers.


    All our programmes have:

  • Small Class Size
  • Personal Attention
  • Strong Foundation Curriculum
  • Innovative and Exciting Learning Methods to Engage Students

Programmes We Offer

Playgroup (2-3 years)

Playgroup offers a platform to children for social interaction, exposure to a new environment, observation, learning, and exploration. Our approach towards teaching involves all these aspects. It promotes a variety of learning methods, which include arts and crafts, music, dance, games, reading, physical activities, and more.


Learn More

Nursery (3-4 Years)

We offer a thoughtfully designed preschool programme which introduces children to new skills and learning techniques and helps achieve the developmental milestones. Our activities are structured to play a crucial role in their growth and to maximize their potential, yet at the same time keep it all fun and games.


Learn More

Jr. Kg (4-5 yrs) and Sr.Kg (5-6 yrs)

This is a transitional phase for children where they’re learning and trying new things themselves. We’ve developed an exploratory programme to support this curiosity and complement their adventures. Our curriculum is composed of theoretical, experiential, and creative classes to deliver a comprehensive learning system to children.

Learn More

Playgroup (2-3 years)

A child’s brain develops rapidly during the early years, forming ways to interpret things, learn, communicate, and connect. This makes playgroup a necessity. Our curriculum is research-based and tested for optimum growth of your child. We focus on all aspects of growth, which include speech development, social interaction, food habits, solution approach, balancing, and more.

    Key developmental areas include -
  • Language and communication
  • Imagination and creativity
  • Brainpower
  • Physical control
  • Social and emotional confidence
  • Learning approach
  • Self-eating habits
  • Preparedness for school routine
  • Confidence to face new environments
The Playgroup Experience.
 A day at Innocent Times’ Playgroup is filled with explorative, exciting activities, games, and fruitful interactions. Our playgroup sessions can be spontaneous or pre-planned; no matter how they are designed, they add value to the day with reading, games, plays, colouring, outdoor activities, delicious and healthy meals, and educational entertainment. At Innocent Times, children develop a lasting learning attitude, strong friendships, and a positive approach.

Nursery (3-4 Years)

Early stimulation is crucial for brain development. Neurons form 700 to 1000 new connections per second in a child’s brain. Interactions at preschool and with parents provide children with the needed social and mental spark.

    Key developmental areas for children.
  • Cognitive and academic development
  • Social and emotional skills like cooperation and resolving conflict
  • Language and literacy - reading, writing, vocabulary
  • Physical development like self-care skills, jumping, running, etc.
  • Creative development - drawing, singing, dancing, etc.

Jr. Kg (4-5 yrs) and Sr.Kg (5-6 yrs)

At this stage, children start to develop a better understanding of their surroundings and create systems to engage with their environment. Our classes provide children with the necessary stimulants and befitting exposure to satiate their inquisitiveness and create a consistent question culture to understand things better.

    Key developmental areas include -
  • Literacy with an understanding of people, places, stories, characters
  • Language development with enhanced writing skills and knowledge of phrases and words
  • Emotional intelligence with a better understanding of feelings, expressions, and reactions
  • Social development with increased interaction with familiar and unfamiliar people, recognition of similarities and differences
  • Physical fitness with more physical play, fine motor skills, and better coordination of body movements
  • Cognitive development - simple maths, pattern creation and recognition, exploration of cultures
  • Creative explorations with participation in activities, plays, musical experiences
