Toddler Care

We follow the highest standards of childcare with research-based programmes. Our environment is designed to facilitate exploration and interaction that a child needs during this time. Our strict hygiene and health policies under the guidance of Dr Ankita Sanghavi ensure that children get optimal nutrition necessary for ideal growth. We have 1:5 caregiver and children ratio, which is recommended for classes with children of this age group.
